WHAT IS THE ZONE

The Zone is a dietary regimen developed by Dr. Barry Sears, focusing on balancing macronutrients (proteins, carbohydrates, and fats) in a 40:30:30 ratio – 40% carbohydrates, 30% proteins, and 30% fats.
Key Principles of the Zone:
Insulin control – by balancing meals, blood sugar levels remain stable, leading to better energy and fat burning.
Anti-inflammatory effect – the correct macronutrient ratio helps reduce inflammatory processes in the body.
Balanced nutrition – emphasis is placed on lean proteins (chicken, fish, eggs), healthy fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts), and low-glycemic carbohydrates (vegetables, fruits).

Benefits of the Zone:
1. Improved energy and focus
2. Effective fat burning
3. Maintenance of muscle mass
4. Reduced risk of chronic diseases

The regimen is not just a diet but a comprehensive way of eating that promotes better health and long-term maintenance of optimal weight.
