WHAT IS THE ZONE

The Zone is a nutritional regimen developed by Dr. Barry Sears, focusing on balancing macronutrients (proteins, carbohydrates, and fats) in a 40:30:30 ratio – 40% carbohydrates, 30% proteins, and 30% fats.
Main principles of the Zone:
Insulin control – By balancing meals, stable blood sugar levels are maintained, which helps improve energy and fat burning.
Anti-inflammatory effect – The proper macronutrient ratio helps reduce inflammatory processes in the body.
Balanced nutrition – The focus is on lean proteins (chicken, fish, eggs), healthy fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts), and low-glycemic carbohydrates (vegetables, fruits).
Benefits of the Zone:
- Improved energy and concentration
- Effective fat burning
- Maintenance of muscle mass
- Reduced risk of chronic diseases
The regimen is not just a diet but a comprehensive way of eating that promotes better health and long-term maintenance of optimal weight.
