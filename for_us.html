OUR STORY - WHY WE STARTED

.

We believe that every new opportunity is a step toward personal growth, which is why we enthusiastically participate in this project, even though we don't feel as educated, steady and ready as others. We're glad for the chance to expand our knowlege, refine our skills and work together. Through teamwork, we have grown closer, inspired each other, and discovered new horizons. We are always ready to learn more, create, and give it our all!

.

WHAT WE FOUND DIFFICULT

The hardest thing we came through was coming together as a team. Since each of us has our own ideas, ways of thinking, and working styles, it led to a few disagreements. Finding common ground, listening to each other, and making decisions together wasn’t be easy to say the least. We compromised, respected each other’s perspectives, and focused on our common goal - this page. We believe a real team is not one that never argues, but one that finds a way to overcome differences and move forward together.
